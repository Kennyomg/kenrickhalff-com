Bizzmark is a company in Nieuwegein that makes websites and applications.

BizzMail is MailChimp competitor, version 1 was made in Phalcon and version 2 with Express.js and React Redux.

BizzBoards is a tool to make dashboards with many APIs integrated with use of Zapier, made in Phalcon with an Express.js API.
